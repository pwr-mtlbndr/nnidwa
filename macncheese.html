Classical Mac 'n Cheese

One of my favorite classical recipes... The best part? It's easy to make and the ingredients are most likely already in your kitchen!

Here's what you'll need:

- ½ cup elbow macaroni (50 g)
- ½ cup water (120 mL)
- 3 tablespoons milk
- salt, to taste
- pepper, to taste
- ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese (25 g)
- fresh chive, to garnish, optional

Preparation

1. Mix the macaroni, water, and salt in a microwaveable mug.
2. Microwave for 2-3 minutes, then stir.
3. Add the milk, cheese, salt, and pepper, then stir.
4. Microwave for another 30 seconds, stir, and garnish with a sprinkle of chives.
5. Enjoy!
